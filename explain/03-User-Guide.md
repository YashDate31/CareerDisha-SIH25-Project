## For Students: Getting the Most Out of CareerDisha

### **üè† Homepage - Your Career Journey Starts Here**

When you first open CareerDisha, you'll see:
- **Career Statistics:** Number of colleges, scholarships, and resources available
- **Quick Actions:** Direct access to main features
- **Navigation Menu:** Easy access to all sections

**What you can do:**
- View overall statistics about available opportunities
- Quick navigate to any section
- Get an overview of what CareerDisha offers

### **üìö Resources Section - Your Learning Hub**

**How to access:** Click on "Resources" in the bottom navigation

**What you'll find:**
- **Educational Videos:** YouTube videos about various careers
- **Articles:** In-depth career guidance articles
- **PDFs:** Downloadable guides and study materials

**How to use effectively:**

1. **Search for Specific Topics:**
   - Use the search bar to find content about specific careers
   - Example: Search "engineering" to find all engineering-related content

2. **Filter Content:**
   - Click "All" to see everything
   - Click "Videos" for video content only
   - Click "Articles" for written content
   - Click "PDFs" for downloadable materials

3. **Read Articles:**
   - Click on any article to open it in a modal
   - Articles include comprehensive information with:
     - Career overviews
     - Salary expectations
     - Required qualifications
     - Career paths and growth opportunities
     - Tips for getting started

4. **Watch Videos:**
   - Click on video resources to watch career guidance videos
   - Videos cover topics like:
     - Career introductions
     - Day-in-the-life of professionals
     - Industry insights
     - Study tips and preparation guides

### **üéØ Career Quiz - Discover Your Aptitude**

**How to access:** Click on "Quiz" in the navigation

**What it does:**
- Assesses your interests, skills, and personality
- Provides career recommendations based on your answers
- Helps you understand your strengths and preferences

**How to use:**
1. Answer all questions honestly
2. Take your time - there's no rush
3. Review your results carefully
4. Use recommendations as guidance, not absolute decisions
5. Retake the quiz if your interests change over time

### **üè´ Colleges Section - Find Your Dream Institution**

**How to access:** Click on "Colleges" in the navigation

**What you'll find:**
- Comprehensive list of colleges and universities
- Information about courses offered
- Location and contact details
- Admission requirements and procedures

**How to use effectively:**
1. **Browse by Interest:** Look for colleges offering your preferred courses
2. **Location Filtering:** Find colleges in your preferred cities/states
3. **Compare Options:** Create a shortlist of 5-10 colleges
4. **Research Further:** Use the information as a starting point for deeper research

### **üí∞ Scholarships - Fund Your Education**

**How to access:** Click on "Scholarships" in the navigation

**What you'll find:**
- Available scholarship opportunities
- Eligibility criteria
- Application deadlines
- Application processes

**Pro Tips:**
- Check regularly for new scholarships
- Apply early - don't wait for deadlines
- Read eligibility criteria carefully
- Keep all required documents ready

## For Parents: Supporting Your Child's Career Journey

### **ü§ù How to Use CareerDisha as a Parent**

**Understanding the Platform:**
- CareerDisha is designed for both students and parents
- The "Parent Guide" section has content specifically for you
- You can explore all sections to understand what resources are available for your child

**Supporting Your Child:**

1. **Explore Together:**
   - Sit with your child and browse the platform together
   - Discuss the career options presented
   - Ask questions about what interests them

2. **Use Parent-Specific Content:**
   - Read articles in the Parent Guide section
   - Watch videos about modern parenting approaches to career guidance
   - Learn about new-age careers you might not be familiar with

3. **Encourage Self-Discovery:**
   - Support your child in taking the career quiz
   - Discuss the results together
   - Respect their interests even if they differ from your expectations

### **üì± Navigation Tips for Parents**

**Getting Familiar with the Interface:**
- The app is designed mobile-first - it works great on phones
- Bottom navigation makes it easy to switch between sections
- Each section is clearly labeled and easy to understand

**Key Sections for Parents:**
1. **Parent Guide:** Your dedicated section
2. **Resources:** Understand what content is available for students
3. **Colleges:** Learn about educational institutions
4. **Scholarships:** Financial support opportunities for your child

## For Educators: Managing Content

### **üîß Admin Panel Access**

**How to access:** Go to http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/

**What you can manage:**
- **Articles:** Add, edit, delete career guidance articles
- **Videos:** Manage video resources and YouTube links
- **PDFs:** Upload and manage downloadable resources
- **Colleges:** Maintain college database
- **Scholarships:** Keep scholarship information updated
- **Users:** Manage user accounts and permissions

**Content Management Best Practices:**

1. **Regular Updates:**
   - Update scholarship deadlines regularly
   - Add new career opportunities as they emerge
   - Keep salary information current

2. **Quality Content:**
   - Write clear, comprehensive articles
   - Ensure all links work properly
   - Use proper formatting for readability

3. **Student-Centric Approach:**
   - Focus on practical, actionable advice
   - Use simple language that students can understand
   - Include real-world examples and case studies

## üîç **Understanding the Content**

### **Article Structure:**
Most articles follow a consistent format:
- **Introduction:** Overview of the topic
- **Main Content:** Detailed information organized in sections
- **Practical Tips:** Actionable advice
- **Resources:** Additional reading or tools
- **Conclusion:** Summary and next steps

### **Video Content:**
Videos are curated to provide:
- **Visual Learning:** Complex concepts explained visually
- **Real Stories:** Experiences from professionals
- **Step-by-Step Guides:** Practical how-to content
- **Industry Insights:** Current trends and opportunities

### **Resource Quality:**
All content is selected to be:
- **Accurate:** Verified and up-to-date information
- **Relevant:** Applicable to Indian students and job market
- **Practical:** Actionable rather than theoretical
- **Comprehensive:** Covering all aspects of career planning
